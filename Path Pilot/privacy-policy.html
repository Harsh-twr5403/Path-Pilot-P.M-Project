Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

PathPilot is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect the following information:

  • Quiz Responses: Your answers to career assessment questions
  • Career Results: Your personalized career recommendations
  • Progress Data: Skill completion and learning goals
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with our platform

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized career recommendations
  • Save your quiz progress and results
  • Track your skill development progress
  • Improve our career assessment algorithm
  • Enhance user experience and platform functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Your data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We don't have access to your personal information
  • Your data is automatically deleted when you clear browser data
  • No server-side storage of personal information

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Tailwind CSS: For styling (CDN)
  • Font Awesome: For icons (CDN)
  • Canvas Confetti: For animations (CDN)

These services may collect anonymous usage data for their own purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data through browser developer tools
  • Delete your data by clearing browser localStorage
  • Retake the quiz and update your results
  • Opt out of data collection (though this may limit functionality)

6. Children's Privacy

PathPilot is designed for college students and young adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use our service without parental consent.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:
